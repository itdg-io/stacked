How does it work?

There are three main parties that must trust each other to make StackEd work.
They are known as a trust triangle.

  • Issuer
  • Holder
  • Verifier
I

The Issuer

The issuer is an entity or organisation that issues credentials or other form of claims to individuals.

Example of issuers:

-   High Schools
-   Universities
-   Organisation Presidents
-   Examination Authority
